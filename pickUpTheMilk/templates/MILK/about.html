About Us

Living in a shared household? Tired of not having a web application that helps you more easily keep track of expenditure for commonly bought/communal items? Bored of your job and need a fresh start in life?

We can't help with that last one, but if you answered yes to the first two then Pick Up The Milk! is for you!

Our platform gives your household a shared shopping list accessable from anywhere you have internet access. We can simplify tracking of purchases and help you record money owed between users. Sign up today!
